Name: Hatsune Kyoko "Koko"

Age: 21 years old

College Degree: Studying psychology

Hitsugaya Kyoko, currently studying psychology in the most renowed university in the entire Japan, Central University of Tokyo (CUT). Koko is the calm and silent type, she tends to analysis someone very closely before speaking to them, which end up influencing her university course's choice. Now at the last semester of her course Koko's teacher asked her to help him in a project about autist people, saying that it would be a great addition to her curriculum, Koko accepted and was designated to study a young man named Suzuki Mambo.

Name: Suzuki Kaito

Age: 25 years old

College Degree: Degree in Mechanical Engineering

Suzuki Kaito, graduated in mechanical engineering in England, had to come back to Japan after a plane crash killed both his parents and now has take care of his young brother that has autism. Now he lives in his old house with his brother and got a job in a famous company named, Robotics Incentive Company (RIC). Without his discernment his brother applied to be an object of study for a project about people with autism and now a student of the Central University of Tokyo(CUT) will come to his house to start the project.

Name: Suzuki Mambo

Age: 19 years old

College Degree: Dropped out High School

Suzuki Mambo, diagnosed with Aspenger Syndrome when he had 14 years old, Suzuki is extremly intelligent when it comes to drawing and perspective and because of that he ends up drawing a lot of robotics projects, part of that is the influence he had from his brother and father who are both Mechanical Engineers. 3 months before beeing diagnosed with Aspenger Syndrome Suzuki stopped going to school and even with his parents asking him to go outside he refused, causing him to drop out from High School.
